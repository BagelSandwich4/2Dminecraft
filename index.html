Our Objective 

The goal in designing this game was to deepen the developers understanding of Python, specifically classes. We did this as students through a course at Olin College of Engineering called Software Design. We implemented the MVC structure; which separates the code into three major categories: Model, View, and Controller. This was achieved through making a fun recreation of the popular game Minecraft in 2 dimensions rather than three. Our game follows the storyline of minecraft including many of the large achievements you receive while completing the game like: mining diamonds, going to the nether, going to the stronghold, and ultimately killing the dragon.
